# FINDASTAY.AI

FINDASTAY.AI aims to disrupt the accommodation booking industry by offering an AI-powered search platform with direct guest-to-business negotiations and a map-based discovery experience. The platform charges a small admin fee instead of traditional 15% commissions, positioning itself as a cost-saving alternative to Booking.com and Expedia. While the concept has merit, execution faces significant technical and market adoption challenges.

## About

The accommodation industry is dominated by OTAs (Online Travel Agencies) like Booking.com and Expedia that charge businesses 15-25% commissions, often costing properties thousands of dollars per booking. These platforms also control customer relationships and create dependency. Simultaneously, travelers face generic search experiences with limited negotiation flexibility and often pay inflated rates due to commission markups passed through pricing. FINDASTAY.AI addresses this by enabling direct negotiations, reducing costs for businesses, and providing an AI-powered discovery experience that could offer better rates to consumers.

## Market Opportunity

Global online travel booking market is $432 billion (2023), with accommodation representing ~65% or $280 billion. OTAs capture ~40% of this, representing a $112 billion market dominated by Booking Holdings ($121B revenue) and Expedia ($95B revenue). In target markets: NZ accommodation market is ~$2.8 billion annually, Australia ~$28 billion, UK ~$45 billion, US ~$180 billion. TAM for our disruption model: $112B globally. SAM (English-speaking markets): $255B. SOM (realistically capturable in 5 years): 0.1% or $255M.

## Tech Stack

React/Node.js stack is solid. Authentication and basic UI already implemented saves time. However, the critical missing pieces are the hardest: real-time inventory management, secure payment processing, dispute resolution systems, and fraud prevention. AI chat integration is straightforward with OpenAI API. Map overlay with pricing requires significant backend optimization. Estimate 6-8 months for MVP with 3-person team, 12+ months for production-ready platform. PCI compliance and international payment processing add complexity.

## Point of Difference

Ultra-low transaction fees (vs 15-25% commissions), AI-powered conversational search, direct guest-property negotiation capability, map-based price discovery interface, and preserved direct relationships between guests and properties post-booking. The combination of cost savings and modern UX could be compelling if executed well.

## Target Users

Primary: Independent hotels, B&Bs, boutique properties, vacation rentals seeking commission relief. Secondary: Budget-conscious travelers, business travelers with flexible requirements, experience-seekers wanting personalized recommendations. Tertiary: Travel managers seeking cost optimization.
