Common Questions

How much can I really save compared to traditional booking sites? +
Properties typically save 85-90% on booking fees (from 15-25% commission to our $0.99-$2.50 admin fee). Many properties pass these savings to travelers through lower rates, while others use it to improve their facilities and services.
Is it safe to book directly through your platform? +
Yes! We use enterprise-grade security and partner with established payment processors. All properties are verified, and we provide booking protection similar to major travel sites, but with the transparency and savings of direct booking.
How does the AI search actually work? +
Our AI processes natural language queries and matches them with property features, location data, and guest preferences. It learns from successful bookings to provide increasingly personalized recommendations while considering your budget and travel style.
What if a property doesn't respond to my negotiation request? +
Most properties respond within 24 hours. If there's no response, you can book at the listed rate or explore our AI-suggested alternatives. We're constantly working with properties to improve response times and availability.
